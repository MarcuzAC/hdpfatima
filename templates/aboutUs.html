About Us

Welcome to the Heart Disease Detection Application. Our goal is to provide an easy-to-use tool for predicting the likelihood of heart disease based on various input features.

This application utilizes machine learning models to analyze user-provided health data and generate predictions. The models have been trained on a diverse dataset to ensure accurate results.

Features of the application:

  • User-friendly interface for data input
  • Accurate heart disease prediction based on input data
  • Easy-to-understand results and recommendations
